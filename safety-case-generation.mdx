## Automated Safety Case Generation

Learn how to use Saphira to automatically generate comprehensive safety cases with evidence and compliance documentation! Saphira provides complete CE Mark Technical File generation capabilities, enabling full preparation for CE Mark and UL Mark certification.

### Start Safety Case Generation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Initiate Safety Case Generation">
    From your project dashboard, navigate to the Safety Case view. Saphira will analyze your risk assessment, control measures, and safety functions to automatically generate a comprehensive safety case.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="file-text" title="Evidence Integration">
    Upload supporting documents, test reports, and compliance evidence. Saphira will automatically link these to relevant safety functions and requirements in your safety case.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Safety Case Components

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Safety Goals and Requirements">
  Saphira automatically extracts safety goals from your risk assessment and maps them to specific safety requirements. Each goal includes:
  - Clear safety objectives derived from hazard analysis
  - Specific performance criteria and acceptance limits
  - Traceability to relevant standards and regulations
  - Evidence requirements for verification and validation
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="link" title="Traceability Matrix">
  The system automatically generates a comprehensive traceability matrix showing:
  - Links between hazards, safety goals, and safety functions
  - Connections to specific standards clauses and requirements
  - Evidence mapping for each safety requirement
  - Verification and validation coverage analysis
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="check-circle" title="Evidence Management">
  Saphira organizes and validates evidence for your safety case:
  - Automatic linking of test reports and validation documents
  - Gap analysis for missing evidence
  - Evidence quality assessment and recommendations
  - Compliance status tracking for each requirement
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Advanced Safety Case Features

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Multi-Standard Compliance">
  Generate safety cases that comply with multiple standards simultaneously:
  - ISO 13849 for functional safety
  - IEC 62061 for safety integrity levels
  - ISO 10218 for industrial robots
  - IEC 61508 for general functional safety
  - Custom standards and regulations
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="file-text" title="Automated Documentation">
  Saphira automatically generates:
  - Executive summaries with compliance status
  - Detailed safety case arguments (GSN format)
  - Evidence summaries and gap analysis
  - Risk assessment documentation
  - Verification and validation reports
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="check-circle" title="CE Mark & UL Certification Support">
  Complete CE Mark Technical Files and UL certification preparation:
  - Full CE Mark Technical File generation
  - UL Mark certification documentation
  - Notified body report generation (UL, TUV, Intertek, Pilz)
  - Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC compliance
  - Low Voltage Directive 2014/35/EU compliance
  - EMC Directive 2014/30/EU compliance
  - Custom regulatory format templates
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Safety Case Workflow

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Initial Assessment">
  Start with your existing risk assessment and safety functions. Saphira analyzes your project data to identify:
  - Safety goals and performance requirements
  - Required evidence and documentation
  - Compliance gaps and recommendations
  - Regulatory approval requirements
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="link" title="Evidence Collection">
  Upload and organize supporting evidence:
  - Test reports and validation documents
  - Design specifications and drawings
  - Risk assessment documentation
  - Compliance certificates and approvals
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="check-circle" title="Generation and Review">
  Saphira generates your safety case and provides:
  - Comprehensive safety case document
  - Evidence mapping and traceability
  - Gap analysis and recommendations
  - Compliance status dashboard
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="file-text" title="Export and Submission">
  Export your safety case in various formats:
  - Complete CE Mark Technical File
  - UL Mark certification package
  - PDF reports for regulatory submission
  - Structured data for further analysis
  - Custom templates for specific authorities
  - Integration with existing documentation systems
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
