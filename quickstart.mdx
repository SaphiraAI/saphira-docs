## Set up your project

Learn how to bootstrap your risk assessment and analyze your system's hazards!

### Import or enter

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="github" title="Import Existing Risk Assessment from Excel">
    Hover over the "Import" dropdown on the top right of the screen and click "Spreadsheet".
    <img src="/images/import-dropdown.png" />
    The spreadsheet should have the following columns:
    <img src="/images/spreadsheet-columns.png" />
    Click "Import" and you're all set!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="rectangle-terminal" title="Enter Hazards Manually">
    Click the "Add Hazard" button on the top right of the Hazards view of the Dashboard tab.
    <img src="/images/enter-hazards-button.png" />
    Enter the hazard details, including the hazard description and the risk
    assessment and click "Create Hazard".
    <img src="/images/enter-hazards-modal.png" />
    You're all set!
    If you'd like to customize the risk graph being applied or the fields being required, you can do so in the Settings tab under "Fields".
    <img src="/images/field-settings.png" />
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
